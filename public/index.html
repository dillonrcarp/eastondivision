The Band

About

East On Division plays the kind of rock and roll that sounds like it was recorded in a barn and mixed in a bar. Rooted in the blues tradition of Clapton and Hendrix, shaped by the raw power of Zeppelin and Pearl Jam, stripped down to the bone by The Black Keys and The White Stripes.

A three-piece out of Muncie, Indiana, bringing a no-frills, high-voltage approach to every stage across the Midwest.
